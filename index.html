About Us

At Glam Spot, we believe that beauty is more than
just a look – it’s a feeling, an expression of your inner self

  • Founded in 2004, Glam Spot was born from a passion for beauty and a commitment to excellence. Our founder, envisioned a space where artistry meets relaxation, where each client is treated as a cherished guest. Over the years, our salon has grown into a beloved local gem, known for our personalized approach and exceptional services.
  • We believe that every client deserves to feel pampered and valued. That’s why we focus on providing exceptional service, using high-quality products, and staying up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in the beauty industry. Our goal is to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere where you can relax and enjoy your time with us.
  • Our team of highly skilled and experienced professionals is dedicated to bringing out your natural beauty. From our talented hairstylists and colorists to our expert estheticians and nail technicians, every member of the Glam Spot family is here to provide you with a customized experience tailored to your unique needs.
